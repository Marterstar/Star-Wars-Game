How to play this game?

It's a game for two players. First player is a Jedi (master Yoda), and he's moving with WASD. Second is a sith (Darth Vader) and he's moving with arrows. Jedi must collect coins, and sith must catch Jedi. If sith catches jedi, he get + 1 score. If jedi collect a coin, he get + 1 score. You have 100 seconds, to collect the highest score. If you want to quit game, press Q.


I wish you a pleasant experience.

If you want to play game and you don't have Windows, you must install Python and PyGame
